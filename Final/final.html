UFO Context

When we think of a UFO, we often imagine things of science fiction, but we do not imagine that this can be a serious subject.

However, during the month of December 2017, the New York Times revealed that between 2007 and 2012, the Pentagon had spent hundreds of millions of dollars to fund a UFO research program (known as the Blue Book Project, between 1952 and 1969 and then known as Glowing Auras and Black Money).

This news surprised many people, but in reality, the UFOs obsession of the American government is very old, and it dates from 1947.

Indeed, that year, Kenneth Arnold – an American aviator – observed a swarm of nine flying objects that he cannot identify and in a few days, all the American press is interested in its history.

In the years that followed, hundreds of Americans also claimed to have seen strange flying objects in the sky and many mistook them for extraterrestrial vessels.

In response to all these testimonies, the US military decided to launch a large scientific study on UFOs which have for main objective to find an explanation to their appearances. And the result is conclusive.

In fact, between 1952 and 1959, the US Air Force recorded twelve thousand incidents involving unidentified flying objects and found a scientific explanation for 97% of these occurrences. However, the possibility that UFOs are the manifestation of extraterrestrial threats continues to make its way and in 1985 President Ronald Reagan said he was even ready to collaborate with the Soviet bloc in case of invasion.

These months, following the revelations of the New York Times, the Pentagon claims that it no longer investigates UFOs but according to Luis Elizondo – the former director of the confidential program – that investigations are continuing today but under another form.

Our objectives

As the Blue Book Project, Glowing Auras and Black Money, we want – through the UFO Sightings data set – answer several questions about extraterrestrial life, but more generally we want to describe and find an explanation to the appearance of UFOs.

Since the article of the New York Times, UFOs are again a hot topic and thanks to 80 000 data of the UFO Sightings data set, we will illustrate all of its data by showing a dynamic world map of all occurrences and also additional visualizations, in order to describe and interpret all the patterns discovered.
